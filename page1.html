Did you know? Some frogs can lay up to 20,000 eggs at once!

Groups of frogs are often referred to as an army. A large group of bullfrogs is called a chorus. Neat, huh!

Frogs absorb water through their skin, meaning they don't usually drink through their mouths!

Some frogs can change color to blend with their surroundings.
